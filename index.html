Data Privacy & Security

Your Privacy is Our Priority: VagalSync is designed as a "thin client" - we never store your health data on our servers. When using VagalSync Free, data is stored locally in your browser. When connected to a MyBodyGuard Guardian, all data remains on your personal device.

We are committed to HIPAA compliance, zero-trust architecture, and giving you complete control over your health information.
